FAQ

Your questions, answered fast

Quick answers to common questions about our student project platform, services, and getting started.

Who can join?

Any student interested in managing, showcasing, or collaborating on academic projects is welcome. No experience needed.

What services do you offer?

We provide graphic design, web development, and other resources to support your projects. See our services page for details.

How do I join a project?

Create an account, browse projects, and request to join or start your own directly from your dashboard.

Is there a fee?

Basic features are free. Some advanced tools or resources may require a subscription, as listed on our pricing page.

Can I collaborate with others?

Yes, you can connect, message, share files, and work together with other students on projects.

How do I get help?

Visit our support page or contact our team. We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
